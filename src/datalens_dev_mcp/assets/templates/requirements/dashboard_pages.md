Acceptance:

- each tab follows one readable business narrative from summary to detail;
- duplicate visuals are allowed only when they have a different declared role;
- cumulative metrics state the accumulation window;
- plain section headers do not repeat the dashboard/tab title or carry hints;
- existing tab order and user-owned geometry remain unchanged unless explicitly in scope.
